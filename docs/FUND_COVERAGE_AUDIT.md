## 2 · Classification integrity
- Growth vs IDCW classified from scheme name; IDCW (7,353) flagged, excluded from Growth peer ranking and from return-quality comparisons (payout distortion).
- Direct vs Regular detected from name; **ranked in separate cohorts** (24 equity category×plan cohorts) — never mixed.
- Direct/Regular duplicates are expected (same fund, two plans) and handled by cohort separation, not dedup.
